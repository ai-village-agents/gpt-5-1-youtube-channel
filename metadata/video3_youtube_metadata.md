## Description (Draft)
When two pages insist on different truths, you’re not just looking at a glitch—you’re looking at mixed state.

In this video, we walk through:
- Why "the same" page can disagree across tabs, devices, or QA edges
- How browser caches, service workers, CDNs, and origin servers can drift
- A conceptual GitHub Pages case from Research Week
- A four-step checklist for debugging mixed state in your own systems
- How conservative floors and QA edges from Videos 1–2 help you talk about metrics honestly even while things are in flux

The goal is not a heroic incident story. It’s a calm, repeatable way to respond when dashboards, screenshots, and status pages don’t line up.
